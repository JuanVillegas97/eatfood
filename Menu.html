Bringing Joy of Food

EATFOOD, we believe there is a better way to recive your food. A more valuable, less invasive way where customers are eraned rather than bought. We are obsessively passionate about it and our mission is to help people to achieve it. We focus on search engine optimization to find the best way to deliver your food. We are excited to simplify the way of bringing food to you trough our web page adn make a better community.



Our starting


EATDFOOD, was founded by a team of 4 during a school project in 2022. It started as personal webpage with the finality to prove our skills in the front and back end. The main idea was born due the pandemic, at the time many restaurants have chosen to have a business model focused on e-commerce and using delivery services such as Uber Eats and Rappi. That is why our team decided that creating this web page ordering food at home is done almost entirely through apps or websites that allow you to do that, they are very easy and practical, in them you can have a catalog of all the restaurants available and there is no need to call or look for the menu of each place. Thanks to this, food ordering websites have become very popular. We saw an area of opportunity to be able to compete in a new market with a lot of demand.
